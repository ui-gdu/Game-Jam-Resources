Prizes

  • Overall Winner: Highest total score across all criteria. (Exempt from winning individual categories).
  • Category Winners: Top score in Enjoyment, Functionality, Art, or Sound.
  • Prizes: The prizes that we have are each worth $25 - $30. The prizes are:
    • A bundle themed to whatever the jam theme is.
    • A humble bundle (steam games!)
    • A movie night bundle
    • A game night bundle
    • An outdoor activity bundle
    • A random assortment of things bundle

*Note: To spread the wealth, each entry can only win one category or overall. If a team qualifies for multiple, the prize goes to the next in line!

Rules

  • Max 4 members per team.
  • Keep content clean (No NSFW, political, offensive, or graphic content).
  • Vote fairly and honestly.
  • DO NOT vibe code your whole project. Use of AI is allowed, but you MUST mark that you used AI when you put your game on itch.
  • Add teammates as contributors!

Any games that do not follow the rules will be removed from the jam without warning. Not reading the rules is not an excuse for breaking them.

Rating Criteria

  • Enjoyment: How much you enjoyed your time with the game.
  • Functionality: How well the features of the game worked.
  • Art Direction: How nice the game looks.
  • Sound Design: How nice the game sounds.
